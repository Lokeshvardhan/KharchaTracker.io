Kharcha Tracker is built with privacy by design. Unlike many apps, we don't collect your data, track your usage, or require internet connectivity. Your financial information remains exclusively on your device.

1. Overview

Kharcha Tracker is designed with your privacy as our top priority. This app operates entirely offline and does not collect, transmit, or share any of your personal or financial data with external servers, third parties, or our company.

2. Data Storage

All your financial data, transaction records, account information, and app preferences are stored exclusively on your device's local memory. This data never leaves your device and remains under your complete control.

3. No Data Collection

We do not collect any personal information, usage analytics, crash reports, or any other data from your use of this app. Your financial information and spending habits remain completely private to you.

4. No Internet Connection Required

Kharcha Tracker functions entirely offline. The app does not require an internet connection to operate and does not communicate with any external servers or services.

5. No Third-Party Services

This app does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, advertising networks, or external APIs. Your data is not shared with any external entities.

6. Device Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, your information's security depends on your device's security measures. We recommend using device lock screens and keeping your device secure.

7. Data Backup and Transfer

Any backup or transfer of your data is handled entirely by your device's operating system (iOS/Android backup systems). We do not have access to these backups.

8. App Updates

App updates are distributed through official app stores. Updates do not change our commitment to local-only data storage and privacy protection.

In Summary

  • No data collection or tracking
  • No internet connection required
  • No third-party services
  • All data stays on your device
  • Complete privacy and control
